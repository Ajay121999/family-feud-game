Press Buzz to Start

🎮 HOW TO PLAY

🔴🔵 Teams: Two teams (A & B) compete.

🔔 Step 1 – Buzz:
Click your team button to buzz in.
The first team to buzz gets control of the question.

💬 Step 2 – Answer:
Type an answer and press ✅ Submit.
If it matches a hidden answer, it will reveal on the board and earn points.

✅ Correct Answer:
Your team keeps control and continues guessing.

❌ Wrong Answer:
Your team gets a strike (❌), but you keep control.

⏱ Timer:
If time runs out, it counts as a strike (❌).
Your team keeps playing unless you reach 3 strikes.

🚨 3 Strikes Rule:
After 3 total strikes, the other team gets ONE chance to steal.

🎯 Steal Round:
The other team gets ONE guess:
• ✅ If correct → they steal ALL points from the round
• ❌ If wrong → original team keeps the points

🏆 Winning a Round:
Reveal all answers OR win the steal to collect points.

⏭ Skip:
Skip a question if stuck (you lose some points).

😂 Fun • 🧠 Logic • 🎲 Risk (double or lose)
🔥 Combo = more points • ⏭ You can skip tough questions
